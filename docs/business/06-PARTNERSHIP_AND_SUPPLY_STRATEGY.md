## Supply Retention Strategy

### Why Owners Leave (and How to Prevent It)

| Reason                                     | Prevention                                                              |
| ------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| "I never get bookings"                     | Improve listing quality, consider price adjustment coaching             |
| "The process is too complicated"           | Simplify listing management, automate availability                      |
| "I had a bad experience (damage, no-show)" | Fast dispute resolution, effective protection plans, compensation       |
| "I don't want the hassle anymore"          | Reduce friction: auto-accept for repeat renters, recurring availability |
| "I sold the item"                          | Nothing to do â€” natural churn. Track as a metric.                       |

### Retention Touchpoints

| Timing                    | Action                                                          |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Day 1 after listing       | Welcome email + "Share your listing" social tools               |
| Day 7, no booking         | "Tips to get your first booking"                                |
| Day 30, no booking        | Personal outreach: "Can I help?"                                |
| First booking completed   | "Congrats! Your first $X earned" celebration                    |
| Monthly                   | "Your earnings this month: $X" summary                          |
| Quarterly                 | "Your Rentify year: $X earned, X items rented, X happy renters" |
| After negative experience | Proactive check-in within 24 hours                              |
